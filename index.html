Weekly Growth Tips

Actionable strategies to optimize your dropshipping store

Optimize Product Listings

Use high-volume keywords in titles and descriptions. Include 3-5 high-quality images per product and write compelling benefit-focused copy.

Social Media Marketing Tricks

Use trending audio clips and hashtags on TikTok and Instagram Reels. Post 3-5 times weekly with user-generated content and behind-the-scenes footage.

Monitor Conversion Metrics

Track conversion rate by traffic source daily. Identify top-performing products and underperformers. A/B test landing pages weekly.

Test Price Points

Run price experiments on new products. Test 3-5 different price points over 2-week periods. Increase prices on bestsellers gradually.

Plan Seasonal Inventory

Plan inventory 60 days ahead of seasonal peaks. Stock up on summer products in April, winter items in August. Monitor competitor trends.
